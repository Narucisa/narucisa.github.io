How To Find GuhCDN

Finding GuhCDN is easy, heres how to know if GuhCDN was on a CollabVM Machine:

  • The VM display is 720p.
  • The VM is either running "My Friend Pingu" or BonziBuddy (or both/none)
  • The VM has a different wallpaper.
  • A Chrome/FireFox/Edge shortcut or desktop shortcut is the Giggity Files Website.
  • The VM Has Opera GX Installed (Windows 10/11 only)
  • The VM Wallpaper is either sponsoring Giggity Files or built in wallpapers
  • Its extremely rare to see guhcdn on vm6 or vm4 (fixtux only)
  • Its extremely common to see guhcdn on vm0b0t, vm1 or vm2 (fixtux only)
